tesla motors’ website re-imagined

minimalistic approach to redesign tesla motors’ website using Sketch App

I discovered Tesla quite recently and I can’t seem to like any other car company ever since. I am a person who loves clean looks, minimalistic designs and TECH. Tesla provides all of those.

So I decided to redesign their already awesome website and give my touch to what I think the company represents. Love the sly naming scheme of the models (S3XY). I’ve tried using a design scheme that reduces the number of clicks and makes browsing a lot easier and convenient.
