re-imagined

redesigning prime video interface to make it look more slick & modern using Sketch App

I had asked my followers on Instagram to give me some ideas they’d like me to design. One of the common suggestions I found was redesigning the Amazon Prime Video User Interface.
I love the work Amazon does but honestly I’m a bit disappointed when it comes to their user interface as compared to its competitors like Netflix and Hulu. So here I have reimagined their user interface from my perspective. Notable changes include:

- an attractive landing page
- bottom navigation with most commonly used tabs
- displaying relevant info upfront to reduce clicks
- displaying duration of episodes upfront to reduce clicks
- maintaining the theme colors of Amazon prime throughout the User Interface
