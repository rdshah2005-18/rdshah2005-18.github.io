Ever since I’ve come to the US, I’ve been ordering a lot of stuff online and UPS has been one of the major service providers for companies like Amazon. Their mobile website and app, however do not seem to reflect their awesomeness.

The idea was to retain the brand colors (ochre and brown) as much as possible, give a more modern look to it, improve reachability and complete it within a few hours.
