In Netflix ‘s show, Mismatched, the character played by Vidya Malavade (known for her role as Vidya from Chak De) suggests an app - Millenial dictionary or something like that so that middle to old age people can understand millenial slangs.

I used that idea and sort of “gamified” it. Each user can learn new slangs everyday or learn using flash cards. In addition to slangs, they can also take tutorials to learn social media apps and services.

The gamification part will be like a quiz or something where they’ll be presented with questions related to the things they’ve learnt. By passing each level they earn points and that will give them gratification and more incentive to learn.

Made a couple of screens for fun!
